Useful Phrases

image: people saying useful phrases Greeting each other
In Chinese culture, people do not tend to hug or kiss each other when meeting people for the first time. Instead, people wave, nod or shake hands. When greeting people, you can also say the following phrases.

title: 你好
pronunciation: nǐ hǎo
meaning: hello
title: 你好吗？
pronunciation: nǐ hǎo ma
meaning: How are you? title: 大家好
pronunciation: dà jiā hǎo
meaning: Hi everyone title: 早上好
pronunciation: zǎo shàng hǎo
meaning: Good morning title: 下午好
pronunciation: xià wǔ hǎo
meaning: Good afternoon title: 晚上好
pronunciation: wǎn shàng hǎo meaning: Good evening
